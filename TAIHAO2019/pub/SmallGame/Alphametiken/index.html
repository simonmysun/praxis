Let's play some traditional puzzles.

In each equation, the decimal digits are represented by other characters. What are they?

  • 一+一+一+一+一=五
  • EINS+EINS+EINS+EINS=VIER
  • 二十一 + 九十九 = 一百二十
  • EINS+EINS+EINS+EINS+EINS=FÜNF
  • ZWEI+VIER+VIER+VIERZIG+VIERZIG=NEUNZIG

Att. There are no leading zeroes in the numbers in the equations. There are no two characters representing a same digit in each equation. The form of answer to submit is as '1+1=2,2+3=5' without quotes.
